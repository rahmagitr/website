Discription :


In this suspenseful thriller, Leeds's relationship with Layla takes a dark turn after a traumatic attack leaves her emotionally scarred. Seeking solace, Leeds forms a connection with another guest, Willow, at the bed-and-breakfast where they first met. As inexplicable occurrences unfold, Leeds is torn between helping Layla and pursuing answers with Willow, risking the well-being of all involved. With tension mounting, Leeds must make a choice that could have devastating consequences for everyone.
