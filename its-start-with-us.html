Discription :


Before It "Ends with Us","It Starts With Us" follows Lily as she navigates coparenting with ex-husband Ryle, but her world is upended when she reunites with first love, Atlas. Despite challenges, Lily embraces a second chance with Atlas, igniting emotional turmoil. Alternating perspectives offer insight into their struggles, love, and sacrifices. Colleen Hoover's poignant storytelling explores resilience, forgiveness, and the enduring power of love, delivering an immersive read that resonates deeply.
