Discription :


In a magical kingdom, Princess Aurora is cursed by the evil fairy Maleficent to die on her sixteenth birthday. To protect her, the good fairy Merryweather alters the curse so Aurora falls into a deep sleep instead. Prince Phillip, aided by fairies, battles Maleficent to awaken Aurora with true love's kiss. The curse is broken, and Aurora and Phillip live happily ever after, uniting their kingdoms. Disney's "Sleeping Beauty" is a timeless tale of love, magic, and overcoming darkness.
