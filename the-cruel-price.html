In "The Cruel Prince," Jude seeks acceptance in the treacherous High Court of Faerie despite her human status. Her intense hatred for Prince Cardan, the most beautiful and wicked of them all, fuels her determination. As she navigates palace politics and her own capacity for cunning, Jude risks everything to protect her sisters and the fragile balance of Faerie.
