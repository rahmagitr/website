"The Fine Print" follows Rowan Kane, a businessman with aspirations of renovating Dreamland, and Zahra, who lands a dream job under challenging circumstances. Their forbidden attraction complicates matters, revealing secrets and challenging their perceptions. As they navigate professional and personal obstacles, they discover that love transcends wealth and status. This book is the first installment in a series centered around three billionaire brothers.
