Discription :


In "The Silent Patient," Alicia Berenson, a seemingly perfect painter, shoots her husband without explanation, becoming a notorious mystery. Criminal psychotherapist Theo Faber is determined to unravel Alicia's silence and uncover the truth behind her actions. As Theo delves deeper into Alicia's psyche, he finds himself entangled in his own motivations and consumed by the search for answers. This gripping psychological thriller explores themes of obsession, deception, and the complexities of the human mind.
