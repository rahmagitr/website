Discription :


In "Icebreaker", Anastasia Allen strives for her Olympic dream at Maple Hills University's figure skating team. However, her path intersects with Nate Hawkins, the hockey team captain, leading to unexpected sparks. Despite their initial friction, Anastasia may find herself relying on Nate to fulfill her aspirations. As they navigate their complex relationship, they confront biases and discover unexpected feelings. This tale explores love, ambition, and the power of overcoming stereotypes.
