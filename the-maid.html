Discription :


Molly Gray, a unique and socially challenged hotel maid, finds herself embroiled in a murder mystery when she discovers the wealthy Charles Black dead in his suite. With the police targeting her as the prime suspect, Molly must navigate a web of deception with the help of newfound friends to uncover the truth. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Molly learns the importance of connection and discovers that all mysteries can be unraveled through the human heart.
