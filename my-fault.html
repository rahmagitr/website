Discription :

In Mercedes Ron's "My Fault," seventeen-year-old Noah's life is upended when her mother marries a billionaire, thrusting her into a world of luxury and danger. Her new stepbrother, Nicholas, epitomizes everything she despises, yet their undeniable attraction complicates matters. As their forbidden romance unfolds amidst secrets and peril, Noah must confront her past while Nick faces a choice that could change everything. This fast-paced YA novel offers a gripping mix of forbidden romance, danger, and electric attraction.
