Discription :


In Colleen Hoover's "Ugly Love," Tate and Miles enter into a no-strings-attached arrangement based solely on mutual attraction. However, as they attempt to keep emotions at bay, they find themselves falling deeper despite Miles's strict rules. Hearts are broken, promises shattered, and love proves to be anything but simple. Hoover's novel explores the complexities of relationships and the inevitability of love's messy reality.
