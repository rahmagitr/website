Discription :


In Jean de Brunhoff's "Babar and His Children," Babar and Queen Celeste await the birth of their first child and are surprised by triplets. The loyal townsfolk celebrate with gifts, and the babies are named and weighed by Dr. Capoulosse. Flora, Pom, and Alexander embark on adventures, needing rescuing by their parents and friends. With charming illustrations, this story captivates young readers and families alike, offering something for everyone.
