Discription :


In Colleen Hoover's "November 9," Fallon and Ben's chance encounter sparks a yearly tradition of meeting on the same date. Their relationship inspires Ben's novel, blurring the lines between reality and fiction. As their bond deepens, Fallon questions if their love is genuine or merely a plot twist. Hoover crafts an unforgettable tale of love, heartbreak, and the power of storytelling.
